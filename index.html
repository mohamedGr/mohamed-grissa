I am currently a PhD student in ECE at Oregon State University under the supervision of Professor Bechir Hamdaoui. I received an M.S. degree in ECE from Oregon State University in 2015 and a Telecommunication Engineering diploma from l'École supérieure des communications de Tunis (SUP'COM) in 2010. Prior to joining OSU, I worked as a Value Added Services (VAS) Engineer with Orange France Telecom Group , Tunis, from 2012 to 2013 and as a Software Engineer at SUP'COM from 2011 to 2012.

Research

I work on the area of security and privacy in computer networks, with current focus on the location privacy issue in wireless networks. My goal is to design and conceive efficient privacy preserving protocols to allow users to enjoy services while maintaining their location information privacy.
